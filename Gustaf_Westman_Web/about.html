Gustaf Westman, established in 2020 by the architect-turned-designer, is a Stockholm-based design studio.

The namesake label presents objects including made-to-order furniture, custom design pieces, ceramics, and glassware , all crafted with a unique aesthetic that blends functionality and beauty. Each product is designed to offer an experience that goes beyond the ordinary, elevating craftsmanship and design to new levels of creativity and quality.

Each made-to-order piece is the result of a direct collaboration with highly skilled Swedish woodworkers, who bring their expertise to create furniture and objects that not only stand out for their innovative design but also for their sustainability. The production process is carried out with a local and responsible approach, using the finest materials that support local artisans and contribute to an environmentally friendly manufacturing cycle. In this way, each piece is not only a reflection of contemporary design but also a commitment to sustainability and ethical production.
